## Template Design Notes

**For Lab Creators:**

1. **Clear goal** - Learners should know exactly what they'll accomplish
2. **Safety first** - Always include relevant safety and ethical notes
3. **Free tools only** - Ensure all tools are accessible to everyone
4. **Step-by-step** - Break down into clear, manageable steps
5. **Examples** - Show concrete examples at each step
6. **Reflection** - Help learners think critically about their experience
7. **Extensions** - Provide optional challenges for deeper learning
8. **Realistic time** - Test the lab and provide accurate time estimates

**Lab Structure:**
* Start with clear objectives
* Safety notes before procedure
* Detailed steps with checkpoints
* Reflection questions for critical thinking
* Extensions for advanced learners
* Troubleshooting for common issues

**Writing Style:**
* Use simple, clear language
* Be specific and actionable
* Include examples and screenshots
* Provide context and explanations
* Encourage experimentation
* Promote critical thinking
