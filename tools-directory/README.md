## üìä How We Evaluate Tools

Every tool in this directory is evaluated against our **5-Star Rubric** across key dimensions:

### Evaluation Rubric

We score tools on a 5-star scale (‚≠ê) across these criteria:

#### 1. **Accessibility** (How easy is it to start?)
- ‚≠ê‚≠ê‚≠ê‚≠ê‚≠ê **Excellent**: No signup required, works immediately in browser
- ‚≠ê‚≠ê‚≠ê‚≠ê **Very Good**: Simple signup (email only), instant access
- ‚≠ê‚≠ê‚≠ê **Good**: Signup with email verification required
- ‚≠ê‚≠ê **Fair**: Complex signup process or waiting period
- ‚≠ê **Poor**: Requires phone number, extensive verification, or waitlist

#### 2. **Free Tier Generosity** (What do you get for free?)
- ‚≠ê‚≠ê‚≠ê‚≠ê‚≠ê **Excellent**: Unlimited or very generous limits (500+ queries/day)
- ‚≠ê‚≠ê‚≠ê‚≠ê **Very Good**: Good daily limits (50-100 queries/day)
- ‚≠ê‚≠ê‚≠ê **Good**: Moderate limits (10-50 queries/day)
- ‚≠ê‚≠ê **Fair**: Tight limits (5-10 queries/day) but still useful
- ‚≠ê **Poor**: Extremely limited (< 5 queries/day)

#### 3. **Quality of Output** (How good are the results?)
- ‚≠ê‚≠ê‚≠ê‚≠ê‚≠ê **Excellent**: Consistently high-quality, reliable results
- ‚≠ê‚≠ê‚≠ê‚≠ê **Very Good**: Usually high-quality with occasional issues
- ‚≠ê‚≠ê‚≠ê **Good**: Decent quality for most use cases
- ‚≠ê‚≠ê **Fair**: Inconsistent quality, requires editing
- ‚≠ê **Poor**: Frequently produces poor results

#### 4. **Safety & Privacy** (Is it safe to use?)
- ‚≠ê‚≠ê‚≠ê‚≠ê‚≠ê **Excellent**: Strong privacy controls, clear policies, no ads
- ‚≠ê‚≠ê‚≠ê‚≠ê **Very Good**: Good privacy, transparent policies
- ‚≠ê‚≠ê‚≠ê **Good**: Basic privacy protections, acceptable policies
- ‚≠ê‚≠ê **Fair**: Some privacy concerns, unclear policies
- ‚≠ê **Poor**: Significant privacy concerns or invasive ads

#### 5. **User Experience** (Is it enjoyable to use?)
- ‚≠ê‚≠ê‚≠ê‚≠ê‚≠ê **Excellent**: Intuitive, fast, delightful interface
- ‚≠ê‚≠ê‚≠ê‚≠ê **Very Good**: Easy to use, responsive
- ‚≠ê‚≠ê‚≠ê **Good**: Functional but basic interface
- ‚≠ê‚≠ê **Fair**: Clunky or confusing interface
- ‚≠ê **Poor**: Frustrating or broken interface

### Overall Rating
The **Overall Rating** is the average of all five criteria, rounded to the nearest half-star.

**What we're looking for:**
