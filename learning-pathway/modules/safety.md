### Setting Up for Safe Family Use

Understanding AI limitations helps you use it safely and effectively.

### AI Cannot Provide Professional Advice

**Medical Questions** âŒ
- Don't ask AI to diagnose symptoms
- Don't use AI for medication advice
- Don't replace doctor visits with AI

**What to do instead:** Call your doctor, visit urgent care, or use official medical resources (CDC, Mayo Clinic).

**Legal Questions** âŒ
- Don't ask AI for legal advice
- Don't use AI for contracts or agreements
- Don't rely on AI for court or legal matters

**What to do instead:** Consult a licensed attorney.

**Financial Questions** âŒ
- Don't ask AI for investment advice
- Don't use AI to make major financial decisions
- Don't trust AI with tax questions

**What to do instead:** Speak with a financial advisor or accountant.

**Mental Health** âŒ
- AI is not a therapist
- Don't use AI for crisis situations
- Don't rely on AI for mental health treatment

**What to do instead:** Contact a licensed therapist or call a crisis hotline (988 Suicide & Crisis Lifeline in the US).

### AI Cannot Guarantee Accuracy

**AI often "hallucinates"** - makes up information that sounds convincing but is wrong.

**Examples of AI mistakes:**
- Citing non-existent books or studies
- Making up historical facts or dates
- Creating fake statistics
- Misunderstanding context or nuance

**Always verify:**
- âœ… Important facts and figures
- âœ… Historical information
- âœ… Scientific claims
- âœ… Anything you'll rely on for decisions

### AI Cannot Replace Human Relationships

**AI should not be:**
- âŒ A substitute for friends
- âŒ A replacement for family time
- âŒ Used instead of talking to parents or teachers
- âŒ A primary source of emotional support

**AI can help with:**
- âœ… Learning and practicing
- âœ… Brainstorming and creativity
- âœ… Organizing and planning
- âœ… Answering factual questions

### AI Cannot Make Ethical Decisions

**Leave these to humans:**
- âŒ What's right or wrong in complex situations
- âŒ How to handle interpersonal conflicts
- âŒ Major life decisions
- âŒ Anything requiring empathy and moral judgment

### AI Cannot Access Real-Time Information (Usually)

Most AI chatbots:
- âŒ Don't know today's news or events
- âŒ Can't browse the web in real-time (except tools like Perplexity)
- âŒ Don't know current sports scores, stock prices, weather
