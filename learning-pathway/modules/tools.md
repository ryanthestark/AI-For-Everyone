The most popular AI chatbot, created by OpenAI. Great for conversations, explanations, writing help, and brainstorming.

- ‚úÖ General questions and learning
- ‚úÖ Writing assistance (emails, stories, essays)
- ‚úÖ Brainstorming ideas
- ‚úÖ Explaining complex topics simply
- ‚úÖ Homework help (with parent supervision)
- ‚úÖ Creative projects

**Free version includes:**

## ‚ö†Ô∏è Tools to Avoid

**For families and beginners, stay away from:**

‚ùå **Uncensored or "jailbroken" AI**
- Designed to bypass safety features
- Can produce harmful content
- Not appropriate for learning

‚ùå **Unknown or sketchy AI websites**
- Might steal your data
- Could be scams
- Unreliable and potentially unsafe

‚ùå **AI tools requiring payment up front**
- Plenty of excellent free options available
- No need to pay as a beginner
- Be wary of subscriptions

‚ùå **AI tools without clear privacy policies**
- Don't know what they do with your data
- Could be collecting personal information
- Stick to reputable companies

## üõ°Ô∏è Staying Safe with AI Tools

### Before Using Any New Tool

**Check these things:**

1. **Is it from a reputable company?**
   - ‚úÖ OpenAI, Google, Microsoft, Anthropic = reputable
   - ‚ùå Random website you've never heard of = risky

2. **Does it require payment information?**
   - ‚úÖ Free tier with no credit card = good
   - ‚ùå Requires credit card for "free trial" = avoid

3. **Does it have a privacy policy?**
   - ‚úÖ Clear privacy policy = better
   - ‚ùå No privacy information = red flag

4. **What's the age requirement?**
   - Most require 13+
   - Kids should use parent accounts

5. **Are there reviews or ratings?**
   - Check what others say
   - Look for red flags in reviews

### General Safety Rules

- ‚úÖ Use official websites (check the URL!)
- ‚úÖ Create accounts with parent email for kids
- ‚úÖ Don't share personal information
- ‚úÖ Turn off chat history if concerned about privacy
- ‚úÖ Use tools on secure, private networks
- ‚ùå Don't download AI tools from app stores without research
- ‚ùå Don't click on AI tool ads without verification
