# Education Directory

## Vision

Welcome to the **Education** section of AI-For-Everyone! This directory is dedicated to providing structured AI education that is:

- **Accessible** - No technical background required
- **Ethical** - Focus on responsible AI usage and understanding
- **Engaging** - Hands-on, practical learning experiences
- **Age-appropriate** - Suitable for learners ages 7+
- **Free** - All resources use free or low-cost tools

## What You'll Find Here

This directory houses our educational curriculum, lesson plans, guides, and resources designed to help anyone learn about AI. Whether you're a student, parent, educator, or simply curious, you'll find:

- **Curriculum Modules** - Structured learning paths from beginner to advanced
- **Lesson Plans** - Ready-to-use teaching materials
- **Ethical AI Content** - Understanding AI's impact on society
- **Learning Activities** - Interactive exercises and projects
- **Assessment Materials** - Tools to track learning progress

## Educational Principles

Our educational content follows these core principles:

1. **Start Simple** - Begin with concepts anyone can understand
2. **Build Progressively** - Layer knowledge step-by-step
3. **Learn by Doing** - Hands-on practice with real AI tools
4. **Think Critically** - Understand AI's capabilities AND limitations
5. **Use Responsibly** - Emphasize ethical considerations

## Ethical AI Education

We believe AI education must include:

- **Transparency** - How AI works and its limitations
- **Privacy** - Protecting personal information
- **Bias Awareness** - Understanding how bias affects AI
- **Critical Thinking** - When to trust (and not trust) AI
- **Human Agency** - Keeping humans in control
