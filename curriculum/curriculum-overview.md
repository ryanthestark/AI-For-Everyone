## ğŸ“š Curriculum Structure

Our curriculum is organized into five core areas:

### 1. [Foundations](./foundations/)
Start here if you're brand new to AI! Learn:
* What AI actually is (and isn't)
* Basic terminology and concepts
* How AI tools work
* Safety and privacy basics
* How to think about AI capabilities and limitations

### 2. [Problem-Solving](./problem-solving/)
Learn how to identify problems AI can help solve:
* Breaking down problems into steps
* Choosing the right AI tool for the job
* When to use AI (and when not to)
* Testing and validating AI output
* Critical thinking skills for the AI age

### 3. [Project Management](./project-management/)
Organize your work and track progress:
* Planning projects (with or without AI)
* Using GitHub Projects and other tools
* Breaking big goals into small tasks
* Tracking progress and staying motivated
* Working with others (collaboration basics)

### 4. [AI-Augmented Development](./ai-augmented-development/)
Learn how to work with AI as a partner:
* Writing effective prompts
* Iterating and refining AI output
* Using AI coding assistants
* Validating AI-generated work
* Best practices for AI collaboration

### 5. [Everyday Life](./everyday-life/)
Apply AI to daily life scenarios:
* Homework and study help
* Household organization
* Creative projects (art, writing, music)
* Learning new skills
* Personal productivity
